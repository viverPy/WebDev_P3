WHO ARE WE?

Jack’s Bar and Kitchen is the brainchild of Ryan and John McElhinney. We are located at 96 Isabella Street (behind Southwark Tube) SE1 8DD. The original Jack’s was opened in Ireland in 1970 by their father Jack so the boys decided to pay tribute to their father by re-opening the original almost forty years on. Jack's is a bar for birthdays, catching up with friends, lunch on our patio in our winter garden, craft beer, cocktails and delicious Thai food.

If you fancy stopping by, propping up the bar and watching our bartenders make the best cocktails in Waterloo, then Jacks is the place to be! If you’re looking for a spot for after-work beers in Southwark then be sure to drop in. Located near the Southbank we have an extensive wine list and authentic Thai menu to match. Our offer extends beyond the pub with takeaway and delivery on our food menu.

Excited for a healthy-ish start to the year after a long month of indulging? We are more than ready for a little health kick. And we think there's no better way to enjoy healthier options than with our full Plant-Based and Better For You Booze menus. It's all about balance, right? Enjoy dishes like our addictive Buffalo Cauliflower - A staple for every share-table. Tender + crispy cauliflower, flavourful buffalo sauce, and creamy vegan ranch dip that will leave you wanting another order.

Though maybe you aren’t looking to share at all. The Mother Earth bowl is hearty and packed full of flavour leaving our bodies and minds feeling full and satisfied. New-ish to our menu, but has already garnered a loyal following. The Mother Earth bowl is made with crisp romaine lettuce + mixed greens, roasted yams + cauliflower, and white cheddar. Topped with addictive quinoa almond crumble, broccoli pesto, and a white balsamic dressing.

Your greens are needed but sometimes you just need a burger + fries. The Impossible Burger is made with thoughtfully-sourced plant-based ingredients so what you eat is good, better for you and better for the earth. (And yes, it's also delicious!) From January 5th - February 1st, enjoy $2 off the Impossible Burger or at Happy Hour when you get an even better deal!
